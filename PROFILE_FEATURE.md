# Profile Page Feature - Complete Summary

## ✨ What's New

### 🎯 Dedicated Profile Page
Click your profile avatar in the sidebar to access your personalized profile page!

### 📊 Statistics Dashboard
Three beautiful stat cards displaying:
- **Favorites Count** ❤️ (Red gradient)
- **Watch List Count** 📚 (Purple gradient)  
- **Total Saved** 📋 (Green gradient) - unique items across both

### 📺 Content Sections

#### ❤️ My Favorites
Grid display of all movies and TV series you've favorited with the heart button

#### 📚 My Watch List
Grid display of all items you've bookmarked for later viewing

## 🎨 Design Features

### Profile Header
- Large profile avatar (100px) with red border
- Gradient background with subtle red tint
- Title and subtitle

### Interactive Stats Cards
- Hover effect: lifts up 4px
- Gradient icon backgrounds
- Large numbers for quick scanning
- Smooth transitions

### Search Integration
When on profile page, search placeholder updates to "Search your collection"

## 🔄 How It Works

1. **Navigate to Profile**: Click your avatar in the sidebar
2. **View Stats**: See counts update automatically
3. **Browse Content**: Scroll through your favorites and watch list
4. **Search**: Use search bar to filter your collection
5. **Interact**: Add/remove items with heart and bookmark buttons

## 💾 Data Persistence

All data persists via localStorage:
- `localStorage.favorites` - favorited item IDs
- `localStorage.bookmarks` - bookmarked item IDs

Your favorites and watch list remain even after closing the browser!
